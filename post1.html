The Future of User and Interface

User and interface are currently separate, but in the future, the lines between the two will likely blend more and more. A metaverse is a network of 3D virtual worlds focused on social connection. The metaverse will create an even more immersive and embodied internet.

Virtual Reality Developments

The CEO of Disney moved to Genies, which is an online virtual platform that focuses on Fashion, Avatars, and the Community. Big influencers are also collaborating with this company, creating avatars such as Cardi B, Migos, and Rihanna.

Possible Ramifications

Creating avatars of self that “live” in the metaverse could have the consequence of users putting out more personal data onto internet, liable for corporations. People may end up giving up their privacy to get more satisfaction with new internet and technological developments.
